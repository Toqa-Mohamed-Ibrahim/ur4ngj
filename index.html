ABOUT US

We Offers Quality Security
Systems & Services

Berimbolo Security is a trusted provider of security solutions for homes and small businesses. With a focus on keeping your property safe, we offer a range of services including security risk assessments, sale and installation of security systems such as alarms and CCTV, as well as system maintenance. Our 24/7 monitoring service ensures that your alarm systems and CCTV footage are always being watched, providing you with peace of mind. Whether you're looking for a basic security package or a custom solution tailored to your specific needs, Berimbolo Security is here to help. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you enhance the security of your property.
